# 05 — Monetization, KPIs & Roadmap

## Revenue streams (in order they typically come online)

1. **YouTube Partner Programme (AdSense)** — unlocks at **1,000 subscribers +
   4,000 public watch hours in 12 months** (or the Shorts equivalent). F1 has
   strong RPMs (engaged, advertiser-friendly, big English-speaking audience).
   *Compliant-first + genuine analysis = monetisation-safe; auto-slop gets
   demonetised — another reason for the chosen setup.*
2. **Affiliate** — F1 games/sims, books, sim-racing gear, merch, streaming
   trials. Natural fit, no audience-size gate.
3. **Sponsorships / brand deals** — once you have a defined, engaged audience
   (often viable from low-tens-of-thousands of subs with good engagement).
   Sim racing, betting (careful — regulation/age-gating), VPNs, gaming brands.
4. **Channel memberships / Patreon** — bonus analysis, predictions, community.
5. **Own products** — merch, a paid newsletter, a season-predictions tool.

> Don't design around money for the first ~6 months. Design around **retention
> and consistency**; revenue follows audience.

## KPIs to watch (by phase)

| Metric | Why | Healthy signal |
|---|---|---|
| **Click-through rate (CTR)** | Packaging quality | ~4–10%+ once established |
| **Average view duration / % viewed** | Content quality, retention | trending up; strong first-30s hold |
| **Returning viewers** | Brand loyalty (the real moat) | growing share |
| **Subs per video** | Packaging × value | positive & rising on hits |
| **Watch hours (rolling 12mo)** | Path to monetisation | toward 4,000 |
| **Search vs Browse traffic mix** | Evergreen foundation vs spikes | both contributing |
| **Cost & time per video** | Pipeline efficiency | falling as templates mature |

Pick 2–3 to optimise at a time. Early on: **CTR + average view duration**.

## Roadmap (0 → 12 months)

### Phase 0 — Setup (Weeks 1–3)
- [ ] Lock name, handle, domain, logo, colours, thumbnail template, intro sting.
- [ ] Choose & lock the **narration voice**.
- [ ] Stand up the pipeline tools (harvest → synthesis → VO → graphics → edit).
- [ ] Build the **reusable graphics/asset library** (tables, gap charts, track
      maps, lower-thirds, stat cards).
- [ ] Write the **script template** + the **research/synthesis prompts**.
- [ ] Produce **ONE pilot video** end-to-end. Pressure-test the whole flow and
      the legal checklist before committing to a schedule.

### Phase 1 — Find the format (Months 1–2)
- [ ] 1 video/week. Mix pillars to see what your specific audience rewards.
- [ ] Iterate titles/thumbnails aggressively; study retention graphs.
- [ ] Bank 3–4 evergreen history/explainer videos as a buffer.
- [ ] Goal: a repeatable look + at least one "what works for us" insight.

### Phase 2 — Consistency & compounding (Months 3–6)
- [ ] 2/week (1 trend + 1 evergreen). Tighten the fast-path race-night playbook.
- [ ] Launch Shorts as top-of-funnel.
- [ ] Start affiliate links; build the email/secondary-platform presence.
- [ ] Goal: cross the **monetisation threshold**; identify your top-performing
      pillar/format and lean in.

### Phase 3 — Scale (Months 6–12)
- [ ] Up to 3/week using the evergreen buffer to cover busy weekends.
- [ ] Streamline the pipeline; consider outsourcing one stage (e.g. editing or
      thumbnails) to protect quality at volume.
- [ ] Pursue sponsorships; explore memberships/products.
- [ ] Goal: predictable cadence, diversified revenue, a defined brand voice
      people return for.

## Risks & mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| **Copyright strikes / Content ID** | Compliant-first (`03`); never use FOM/broadcaster footage; original graphics as backbone. |
| **YouTube "inauthentic content" demonetisation** | Human gates, original angle + original graphics, real analysis — not auto-slop. |
| **Generic/derivative output** | Synthesis-not-paraphrase prompts; the script human-gate kills bland drafts; one non-obvious angle per video required. |
| **Factual errors (LLM hallucination)** | Verify every fact against a primary source at the script gate. |
| **Defamation in drama pieces** | Sourced facts only; opinion clearly flagged; no unproven claims (`03`). |
| **Burnout / cadence collapse** | Evergreen buffer; sustainable cadence; batch in quiet weeks; outsource a stage in Phase 3. |
| **Trend dependence** | Evergreen foundation + owned email/secondary audience. |
| **Single-voice/tool lock-in** | Keep raw scripts/VO; document the pipeline so stages are swappable. |
| **Platform risk (one channel)** | Repurpose to newsletter/TikTok/X/Reddit; own the audience off-platform. |
